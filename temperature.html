  Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes signifigantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). However once you get within about 20 degrees of the equator the change in temperature flattens out dramatically. Even more interestingly, is the fact that it gets colder faster in the northern hemisphere compared to the southern hemisphere. This is possibly due to the season and tilt of the earth at the time of analysis. Additional studies would be necessary to find out if that trend remains the same or reverses in different seasons.
