  Wind Speed

The most evident thing from the wind speed analysis is that the majority of locations in our sample had 10 mph wind speeds at the time of the test. I think that it's likely that trend carries over to the whole population of all cities and latitudes. Another observation from this sample is that only the northern hemisphere has any locations with wind speeds over 25 mph. There is a much lower chance that this observation is true of the world as a whole, but it is unknowable either way from this data set and would require a much larger sample.
